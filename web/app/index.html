GPS Tracking

Volvo truck engines are transported from Skövde to Göteborg, where they will be installed into brand new Volvo trucks.

By adding a GPS transmitter to each engine carrier, Volvo can immediately detect delays and bottlenecks along the way and adapt accordingly.

Supply-chain delays typically trigger a chain reaction of events, least of all financially.
Every truck has a buyer. By associating engine transports with their purchase orders, supply chain events immediately reflect on Volvo Treasury's liquidity forecast.
